Services

Family day care

We offer education and care services by registered educator with diploma qualification and 3 years of experience in a long day care centre. Our educator is supported by Integricare Family Day Care St George. The environment is setup for early childhood learning to create an atmosphere that is safe, welcoming and engaging. It helps the children develop their skills, interests and become independent and confident learners. We are approved to administer Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Child Care Rebate (CCR).

Opening: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 6:00PM
Group age: 10 months - 5 years old

Before and after school care

Our long and flexible opening hours between 7:30AM to 6:00PM Monday to Friday with a great location of just behind the Bexley Public School make it the ideal place to establish a before/after school care service in the area. To find out more, come and speak to our experience educator.

Opening before school: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 9:00AM
Opening after school: Mon - Fri 3:30PM - 6:00PM
Group age: School age

Vocation care

At Pineapple Club, we accommodate for parent who require child care for a non-casual, ad hoc basis during the day or after working hours. Saturday and Sunday care can be arranged. We have a strong and fun educational activities that your child will enjoy. So contact us to discuss about your needs, we are confident that our experience educator will be able to assist you.

Opening: Mon - Fri 7:30AM - 6:00PM
Sat - Sun can be arranged
