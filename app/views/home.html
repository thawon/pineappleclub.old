Welcome

Pineapple Club Family Day Care is a Family Day Care (FDC) registered with Integricare Family Day Care St George. Our FDC is operated by a trained educator who has years of experience in long day care centre with diploma qualification. We are approved to administer Child Care Benefit (CCB) and Child Care Rebate (CCR)

Our FDC key features

  • 90% of the space is setup for childcare service
  • Educator is supported by high quality FDC organisation Integricare with every need
  • Children receive more attentions due to a low child-educator ratio. Children will better develop and bond with educator
  • Our diploma qualified educator has years of experience providing care in long day care centre
  • Strong educational programs
  • Activity report is provided to parent on daily basis
  • Live photo of the child is sent to parent via a private mean of communication
